

## Plan: Planetary Orbit Popup for Categories

### Concept
Replace the accordion dropdown with a **popup overlay**. Tapping a category (e.g. "Stay Healthy") opens a centered modal with:
- **Blurred backdrop** covering the screen
- The **category icon as the "planet"** in the center (larger, glowing)
- **Child features orbiting around it like moons** in a circle, animated in with staggered spring animations
- Tap a moon to navigate; tap backdrop or X to close
